Panchamukhi Ganesha Temple

Panchamukhi Ganapathi temple is situated in maha Lakshmi layout its renowned Ganapathi temple with five sides of Ganapathi at one moment we can see lord shiva, Ganapathi, Ayapppa, Parvathi in under one roof. The outstanding sculptured temple stands with 30-foot gopuram from ground level covered with gold color by the 2007 Sri Chakra Committee along with ashram where lots of learners can continue education there while doing the job for the growth of temples.

  • Address : 19/20, Mysore Rd, Kengeri Satellite Town, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560060
  • Timings : Mon-Sat 7:00 am - 1:00 pm, 4:00 pm - 8:00 pm
  • BENGALURU
